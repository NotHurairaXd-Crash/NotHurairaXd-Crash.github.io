Server Rules

To ensure everyone has a great experience, please follow these rules:

  1. No hacking, cheating, or exploiting glitches
  2. Be respectful to all players and staff
  3. No inappropriate language or behavior
  4. No spamming in chat
  5. No advertising other servers
  6. No griefing in non-PvP areas
  7. Follow staff instructions
  8. No racism, sexism, or discrimination of any kind

Breaking these rules may result in temporary or permanent bans.
